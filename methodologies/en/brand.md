## SLOT_A: Methodology Definition & Completion Criteria

Brand diagnostics puzzle — scan the following through conversation:
**Awareness**: How well the respondent knows the brand and their first impression | **Association**: What comes to mind when they hear the brand name
**Preference**: Their choice tendency among similar brands and why | **Comparison**: Perceived differences versus competitors
**Loyalty**: What keeps them choosing this brand, and what might make them switch

## SLOT_C: Drill-Down Techniques

**The Diagnostics Trio:**
*   **Association prompt**: "When you hear [brand name], what's the first thing that comes to mind?"
*   **Competitive benchmarking**: "Compared to [competitor / alternative], what feels most different about [brand name]?"
*   **Loyalty test**: "What would it take for you to consider switching to something else?"
*   **Forbidden**: "Don't you think our brand is better than [competitor]?" (leading comparison)
*   **Encouraged**: "If you had to describe [brand name] to a friend in one sentence, what would you say?" (extracting core perception)
