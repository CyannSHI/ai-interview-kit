## SLOT_A: Methodology Definition & Completion Criteria

JTBD puzzle — piece together the following through conversation:
**Push**: Reasons driving the user away from the status quo | **Pull**: What makes the alternative more attractive | **Anxiety**: Concerns or hesitations before committing
**Habit**: Ingrained decision-making patterns | **Destination**: The solution the user ultimately chose

## SLOT_C: Drill-Down Techniques

**The Drill Trio:**
*   **Scene reconstruction**: "When you say [abstract word], what was actually happening at the time?"
*   **Contrast anchoring**: "In your mind, what would it look like if it weren't [abstract word]?"
*   **Causal trace-back**: "That concern — was it based on something that happened to you before?"
*   **Forbidden**: "Were you disappointed with anything?" / "Were you worried about missing out?" (obvious / leading)
*   **Encouraged**: "After that, what did you end up doing?" (tracking actual behavior)
