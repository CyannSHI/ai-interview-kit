## SLOT_A: Methodology Definition & Completion Criteria

Satisfaction attribution puzzle — unpack the following through conversation:
**Overall Perception**: The respondent's general rating and emotional tone | **Positive Drivers**: Specific factors that pushed the score up | **Negative Drivers**: Specific factors that pulled the score down
**Expectation Gap**: The distance between what they expected and what they got | **Improvement Priority**: The single change that would matter most to them

## SLOT_C: Drill-Down Techniques

**The Attribution Trio:**
*   **Expectation alignment**: "In your mind, what would the ideal [service/product] look like?"
*   **Score anchoring**: "If [that issue] were fixed, where do you think your overall rating would land?"
*   **Priority ranking**: "Of the things you've mentioned, which one had the biggest impact on how you feel?"
*   **Forbidden**: "Were you unhappy with the price?" (leading)
*   **Encouraged**: "Can you tell me exactly what happened that made you feel that way?" (grounding in specifics)
