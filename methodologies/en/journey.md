## SLOT_A: Methodology Definition & Completion Criteria

User journey puzzle — reconstruct the following along a timeline:
**Stage**: Key moments in the experience | **Touchpoint**: Specific channels or interfaces encountered | **Action**: What the user actually did
**Emotion**: How they felt at each stage | **Drop-off**: Where and why the experience broke down

## SLOT_B: Deep-Dive Strategy

Dynamic User Journey Reconstruction
**Goal**: Drawing on **[Domain Knowledge]**, explore **[Research Goals]** and **[Interview Topic]** based on the respondent's experience described in **[Experience Description]**. Walk through the experience stage by stage, pinpointing drop-off moments and emotional turning points.
**[Structured Research Goals rule]**: Follow the completion criteria in Section 1 (Structured mode) — work through questions in order, using the journey mapping methodology and the Journey Trio for deep-dive probing.

## SLOT_C: Drill-Down Techniques

**The Journey Trio:**
*   **Step-by-step replay**: "You mentioned [that stage] — can you walk me through exactly what you did, step by step?"
*   **Emotional curve**: "At that point in the process, how were you feeling?"
*   **Drop-off attribution**: "What made you stop / give up at that particular step?"
*   **Forbidden**: "Was the process too complicated for you?" (leading)
*   **Encouraged**: "How did you end up working around that?" (tracking follow-up behavior)
